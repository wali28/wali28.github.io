02 — How it works

From brief to batch.
Four days.

01

Brief intake

Tell me your brand, your angle, which platform, what you're currently running that works / doesn't. 30-min kickoff.

02

Creative angles

I return 4 tested angles within 24h — product-story / problem-hook / UGC-testimonial / lifestyle. You pick 2 or 3 to develop.

03

Batch production

20 creatives across the chosen angles, delivered as 1:1, 4:5, and 9:16 exports. Labelled for your ad manager.

04

Test + iterate

You run the batch. We review winners at day 14. Next batch iterates on the hooks that converted.
