04 — How it works

Four steps.
No weird stuff.

First message to handover. Most projects finish in 1–3 weeks.

01

Intro call

30 minutes. Tell me the problem. I'll tell you honestly if I'm the right person — and if not, who probably is.

02

Scope & quote

Fixed price, fixed deliverable, fixed timeline. Proposal back within 48 hours. No hourly games. If scope shifts, we re-quote explicitly.

03

Build

Async by default. Shared doc, progress notes every day, two check-in calls per week. You see work evolving, not silence then a big reveal.

04

Handover

Final files, a Loom walkthrough explaining everything, and one revision round included. Then it's yours.
