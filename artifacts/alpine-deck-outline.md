## Investor objection prep

**Q: "Isn't this a saturated market?"**  
Saturated for heritage. Open for design-forward DTC. Rapha proves the premium tier works — but at €250 they left €120-180 uncontested. That's our slot.

**Q: "How do you compete with Decathlon on price?"**  
We don't. They sell €60 starter kits. We sell €180 serious-enthusiast kits. The enthusiast segment is 40% of spend, 15% of buyers — we'd rather own it than chase the long tail.

**Q: "Inventory is a capital pit. Why DTC vs wholesale?"**  
DTC keeps GM at 78%. Wholesale would drop us to 40-45%. We're planning a small (10-15 door) specialist wholesale program Y2, but only with pro shops who won't discount us.
