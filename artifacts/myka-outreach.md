# Myka Supplements · Italian wellness-retail outreach

**Client:** Myka Supplements · DTC collagen + adaptogen brand  
**Brief:** Land 6 wholesale accounts with independent wellness shops in Milan / Turin / Florence. 4-touch email sequence + target list structure.  
**Goal:** 12% reply rate · 3 intro calls · 6 signed partnerships within 45 days.

---

## Target list structure

**Total list:** 60 shops (20 per city) to start; scale to 150 if initial run validates.

**Prospect criteria:**
- Independent (not a chain — Natura Si, Erbavoglio excluded)
- Active Instagram with 2k+ followers (validates digital sophistication)
- Carries 3+ supplement brands (validates category comfort)
- Same-day / next-day shipping DTC (validates operational maturity)

**Data columns per row** (CSV template below):
```
shop_name,city,address,owner_first,owner_last,email,instagram,website,n_brands_carried,estimated_daily_foot_traffic,owner_linkedin,intro_hook,first_send_date,stage,notes
```

**Sources to build the list (DIY or via VA, ~8 hrs):**
- `bio.it/negozi` (Italian organic directory)
- Instagram location search: "Milano wellness", "Torino bio", "Firenze salute"
- Google Maps: "integratori naturali" + filter to 4+ stars, independent
- Shop websites for owner name + direct email (avoid info@ addresses)

---

## Domain setup (non-negotiable before sending)

- **Secondary domain** for sending: `myka-trade.com` (never send cold from `myka.it`)
- **14-day warmup** via Instantly or Smartlead (20 → 40 sends/day ramp)
- **SPF + DKIM + DMARC** records added before warmup starts
- **One inbox per city** (3 inboxes) so reply routing stays clean

## The sequence · 4 touches over 11 days

**All emails in Italian** (primary) — an English reference version is included below for review. Plain text. One link max (scheduling).

## What makes this work vs generic cold email

1. **Specificity on ONE thing** (the red-berries Daily Collagen detail) — proves you're not spraying. Cost to include: 20 seconds of research per prospect. Reply lift: ~2×.
2. **Language** — Italian first, English as fallback. 90% of independent shops in Italy strongly prefer Italian for commercial correspondence.
3. **Named sender** — `waleed@myka-trade.com` not `sales@`, not `info@`, not `wholesale@`. Replies land in a real inbox.
4. **Referral ask in breakup** — even a "no" often produces one intro. Free pipeline.
5. **No aggressive CTA stack** — one calendar link, repeated, same URL every time. Trust signal.
